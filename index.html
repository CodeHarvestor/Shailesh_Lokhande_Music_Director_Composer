About Me

I’m Shailesh Chandra Lokhande, a freelance music director with 10+ years of experience in composing soulful, powerful, and catchy tunes. From devotional and inspirational songs to political campaign anthems, item numbers, and commercial jingles—I bring every genre to life. Music, for me, is emotion in motion—crafted to move hearts, minds, and feet. Every track I create tells a story and leaves a lasting echo. Let’s make music that matters.

Work Profile

Bappa Morya Re · By Shankar Mahadevan
“Bappa Morya Re” is a heartfelt tribute to Pune’s most revered Ganpati – Shrimant Bhausaheb Rangari Ganpati, the deity with whom India’s first public Ganeshotsav began. Celebrating history, devotion, and culture, this song beautifully blends traditional fervor with a modern musical touch.
